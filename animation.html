About

My name is Victor and I am 20 years old. I am Italian-Belgian and I was raised in Rome.

I have always been passionate about storytelling: from filling out notebooks with drawings of original stories to my love for cinema and acting.

I entered the world of animation because I felt that being able to create anything from the ground up would be the most rewarding way to express my creativity. I studied CG Animation at IED Rome, and I am now enrolled in School of Visual Arts (SVA)'s MFA in Computer Arts.
